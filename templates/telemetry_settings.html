Privacy First

All telemetry data is anonymized. We never collect your documents, queries, personal information, or business data. Only technical diagnostics to help us fix bugs and improve performance.

Settings
Crash Reporting Recommended

Automatically send error reports when something goes wrong. Helps us identify and fix issues quickly.

What's collected?
✓ Collected:
  • Error type and message (scrubbed of personal info)
  • Stack trace (code location of error)
  • App version and operating system
  • What you clicked before the error (breadcrumbs)
✗ Never collected:
  • Your documents or queries
  • Usernames, emails, or passwords
  • Database content or connection details
  • API keys or license keys
Usage Analytics Optional

Share anonymous usage statistics to help us understand which features are most valuable and where to focus improvements.

What's collected?
✓ Collected:
  • Feature usage counts (e.g., "agent created")
  • App version and subscription tier
  • Success/failure rates for operations
✗ Never collected:
  • What you name your agents or workflows
  • Content of your prompts or queries
  • Any identifiable information
Performance Monitoring Optional

Share timing information to help us identify slow operations and optimize performance.

What's collected?
✓ Collected:
  • Operation durations (e.g., "agent response took 2.5s")
  • Performance percentiles across users
Settings auto-save
Why collect telemetry?

Telemetry helps us identify bugs you might not even notice, understand which features matter most, and ensure new versions are stable before reaching everyone.

Your data stays yours

All identifiers are one-way hashed. Even we can't trace telemetry back to you. You can opt out completely at any time.
