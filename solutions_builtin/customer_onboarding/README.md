# Customer Onboarding

A ready-to-run solution that turns a pile of customer documents into a
populated customer requirements spreadsheet, guided step-by-step by AI.

## What's included

- **Workflow** — *Customer Onboarding AI Guided v4 (with Bulk Update)*. A
  7-node workflow that reads application forms, shipping requirements and
  compliance docs, extracts the structured fields, and bulk-updates a
  Customer Requirements Excel template.
- **Sample inputs** — anonymised PDFs and the blank Excel template so the
  workflow can run end-to-end without any real customer data.

## For end customers

After installing, click **Run the onboarding workflow** on the post-install
screen. Drag in one or more customer documents and the blank template — the
workflow will do the rest and hand back a filled-in spreadsheet.

## For demos

Everything needed is bundled. No connections, no credentials. The post-install
button kicks off the workflow against the included sample inputs, so the full
"before & after" story can be shown in under two minutes.

## For consultants

This is a reasonable starting point for most document-to-spreadsheet customer
onboarding flows. The workflow is tenant-owned after install, so you can
rename it, swap the template for a client-specific one, and layer additional
nodes on top without any platform changes.

Edit `branding.json` before repackaging to give the tile and wizard a
client-specific display name, tagline and accent colour.
