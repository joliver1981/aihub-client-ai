ACME Corporation — Employee Handbook (Summary)

Section 1: Remote Work Policy
All employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work days must be pre-approved by the direct manager at least 48 hours in advance. Employees must be available on Slack during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM EST). Home office equipment stipend:  per year.

Section 2: PTO Policy
Full-time employees receive 20 days of PTO per year, accruing at 1.67 days per month. PTO requests for 3+ consecutive days require 2 weeks advance notice. Unused PTO carries over up to a maximum of 5 days. PTO is not paid out upon termination during the first year of employment.

Section 3: Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses over  require pre-approval from a manager. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of the expense. Travel expenses are reimbursed at GSA per diem rates. Software subscriptions require IT department approval.

Section 4: Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted quarterly. Self-assessments are due 1 week before the review meeting. Rating scale: 1 (Needs Improvement) through 5 (Exceptional). Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for the annual bonus pool.
